Whale Donut Burger

Here's what we'll be making:

"This whale burger is one of my family's favourite recipes. I invented it last week when I was thinking about what to make for dinner as I drove home. Ahead of me was a flat-bed truck carrying a recently deceased bowhead whale that had been found washed ashore. The gasses inside its festering corpse built to such a preasure that it caused the whale to explode. I scraped some of the remains off my windshield and voila! Dinner!" -A.K.

Ingredients

4 kg ground Reclaimed whale meat
9 cups Canola oil
1 large Honey-glazed donut
2 sliced Yellow onions
1 cup Honey mustard
4 cups Grated cheddar cheese
2 cups Barbecue sauce
Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Add oil to a large fry pan. Heat until smoking.
  2. Add onion to pan and stirfry until soft.
  3. Add whale meat, stirring constantly until browned.
  4. Meanwhile, slice donut horizontally and place under broiler for five minutes.
  5. Once donut is cooked, add barbecue sauce to the lower half and then add half of the meat. Top with cheddar cheese and place under broiler until cheese is melted.
  6. Remove from broiler and add the rest of the meat and drizzle the honey mustard on top. Top with the remaining donut half and serve.
