Whale Stir Fry

This whale stir fry is one of my family's favourite recipes. I invented it last week when I was thinking about what to make for dinner as I drove home. Ahead of me was a flat-bed truck carrying a recently deceased bowhead whale that had been found washed ashore. The gasses inside its festering corpse built to such a preasure that it caused the whale to explode. I scraped some of the remains off my windshield and voila! Dinner!

Here's what we'll be making:

Ingredients

Once you've added an ingredient, click on it to remove it from the list.

4 kg Whale meat
3/4 cup Canola oil
1 cup Jasmine rice
14 Snow peas
3 Bell peppers, any colour
1/4 cup Bean sprouts
2 cups Soy sauce
1 cup Paprika

Instructions

  1. Cook rice according to package directions.
  2. Heat oil in wok and add whale, stirring constantly for about 10 minutes.
  3. Add snow peas, bell pepper, and bean sprouts.
  4. Stir in soy sauce and paprika. Mix and serve over rice.
